Who I Am

In the real world I inhabit Phoenix, Arizona, USA. Between five and ten weekday evenings I can typically be spotted near N33° 29.0744′, W112° 5.3194′.

When I’m not there you can find me online, often lurking in an IRC channel, reading or writing documentation, forking projects and pushing commits to Github , or digging through the depths of information scattered throughout the Web. If all else fails, look for me at the dog park reading an analog book near a golden and a catahoula.

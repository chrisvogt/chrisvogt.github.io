In the real world I inhabit Phoenix, Arizona, USA. Between five and ten weekday evenings I can typically be spotted near N33° 29.0744′, W112° 5.3194′.

The end of 2013 I came down with a touch of lymphoma and spent fourteen months married to a chemo pump. Cancer patients have a generous amount of time to spend reading and learning, and I remain an assiduous reader to this day. I also frequently play tunes on the piano.

Fall 2014 I returned to school full time for discipline, structure, and networking, and in 2016 will likely transfer somewhere to study engineering or philosophy. I have been in full remission since January and now sport hair and a handsome beard.

Prior the disruption, my professional endeavors have been in eDiscovery, EdTech, and Health Care as a web developer and engineer. I am a strong proponent of the Open Web Platform and actively contribute to and maintain projects written in JavaScript, PHP, and Python int. al.

For now, I remain poised to rise to the occasion.
